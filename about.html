About Us

We are a group of innovators and solution providers who could sense the requirement of integration of e-Com applications onto POS terminals with edge computing solutions.

We have done extensive refinement on our POS terminal applications with respect to the customer requirements and are willing to customize applications for the unique business processes.

We have wide range of open-source applications placed on POS terminals suiting to the current requirements of market.

GL Pos Billing

A solution that generates both invoice/bill and receipt against an order at any location including restaurants, stores, super market, sales counters

GL Pos Token

A token based ticketing solution for collection of revenue from your customers on fist come first serve basis

GL Pos Pooja

A solution for selecting the pooja offering as well as collection of donation / annadhanam from pilgrims for temples/ places of worship from any location

GL Pos Bus

A solution for generation of customized bus ticket for any bus, any route against any preset fares between stops/ locations/ towns
